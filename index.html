Designer & Developer

Hello, I'm Alfredo Barillas, a Designer and Front-End Developer specializing in Web Design and Development.

I am a self taught developer, I have learned how to code by the best instructors on Udemy as well as being chosen to be in the Grow with Google Scholarship by Udacity in collaboration with AT&T. Being chosen in this scholarship really made me confident in my skills as a designer and a developer. Upon receiving the scholarship I have taken on some freelance work with some local clients in Southern California.

As I first started to learn how to design and develop websites it was no easy task but coding and creating was something that I have always loved to do, so when I found out about coding and further researched more about it I love that I could create anything my mind desires. I would wake up every day excited to learn but I knew if I wanted to be good I would need to learn and study as much as I can and that is what I did, I grinded and I am now confident to finish any design or task given to me. I am eager to develop my skills as a developer and to reach my goal of becoming a full-stack developer.

I am always ready to learn new programs and techniques needed for any project and retain as much information as i can about what I am passionate about.
